ALABAMA BUHURT

Have you ever thought about putting on medieval armor and testing yourself in battle? Buhurt could be the thing for you! Buhurt is a full-contact medieval combat sport where participants wear historically accurate armor and use blunt steel weapons to engage in battles. Originating from historical reenactments, it has evolved into a competitive discipline featuring team melees and individual duels. Matches are intense and physically demanding, with the goal often being to knock down or disable opponents. Popular in Europe and growing worldwide, buhurt combines history, sport, and spectacle.
